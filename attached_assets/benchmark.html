Verdict

Where SkyGuard wins, where it doesn't, and what to do about it.

💰
Price — We Win Hard
~$2,500 vs $21,000–$430,000. Same core detection for 10–170× less. The hardware is commodity — the competitors sell support contracts and brand logos.
🖥
UI & Spectrum — We Win
Real-time radar + waterfall in a clean web UI. Most competitors have UIs that look like 2012. Mobile-friendly out of the box.
🔒
Self-hosted — We Win
Dedrone requires cloud. We work air-gapped. For clients with data sensitivity (military, gov, corporate) — this is a major differentiator.
🎯
Direction Finding — Roadmap
$100–250 of additional hardware + a few weeks of work. Yagi antenna + servo for Level A. Brings us to parity with most mid-tier systems.
🤖
RF Fingerprinting — Roadmap
Public datasets exist (AirID, CardRF, UAVSig). Self-learning AI via Remote ID auto-labeling. Not months from scratch — weeks with existing data.
📋
Certifications — Gap
No CE/FCC product cert. Not needed for private clients. Required for government/military procurement. Not a priority for SMB market entry.
